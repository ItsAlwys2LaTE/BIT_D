VISTA CLUB

It has earned remarkable recognition from the college in very short span. The VISTA club organizes various cultural events for the students from all batches and branches together.

The club channels the immense creativity of students in our college and keeps them mentally stimulated while enjoying a well-deserved breathing space in between versatile technical sessions in the curriculum. It gives very good refreshing fragrances to both participants and volunteers that will certainly enhance confidence, personality, teamwork, and learning by experience. OBJECTIVES

To gather our students from culturally diverse communities together, providing them opportunities on various platforms to engage from every walk of life in unique educational and cultural opportunities that reflect and celebrate our heritage.

In finding the hidden artists and giving them the requisite limelight, VISTA Club aims:

  • To provide a platform for the students to show their hidden talent

  • To promote UNITY IN DIVERSITY within the college

  • To gain recognition in various fields apart from academics

  • To gain confidence in the long run for the personality development of participants

EVENTS

the VISTA club has five wings under its flag namely, elocution, dramatics, dance, music, and fine arts. These wings conduct regular activities like debates, speeches, quizzes, essay writing, etc. deliberately hence engaging students to make full use of their idle time. Apart from these, participants are also trained in the medium of workshops and guidance from experts.

  • Debate competition and essay writing is organized to give a platform to young minds to showcase their talent.

  • Soft skills seminar was conducted by Vista, to enable students to develop their communication skills.

  • Motivational lectures by eminent personalities are being organized from time to time.

  • The induction program of first-year students is organized, to give them a brief outlook of the college and the years to come.

  • Additionally, students from our college have represented BIT in various national-level fests like Mood-Indigo, Kshitij, Aarush, Aavartan,

  • Rendezvous, and national-level MUNs at IIT BHU and KIIT.
