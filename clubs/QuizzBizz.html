QUIZZBIZZ

We are the official student-led quizzing club of BIT-DURG,under the Training and Placement cell. Develop and foster an environment of knowledge sharing and learning. Help students to develop their overall personality by giving them an edge while tackling future competitions. Building a community valuing knowledge across different fields. Execution of innovative ideas while fostering the growth of individuals. Basically, we’re the Jack of all Trades and our main goal is to ‘end our day being a little smarter than we started.’

OBJECTIVE

To provide students with a platform where all types of innovative ideas are welcomed and fostered until they are prepared for the betterment of society.
